What platforms should your app run on?

Will your app run on iOS or on Android? There are benefits to prioritising certain platforms. Android has the wider audience reach but users tend to be less engaged whereas iOS has a more narrower user base but they tend to be more engaged.

What kind of login should the app use?

Your app does not have to have a login but in some cases where you would like to maintain a relationship with your users and track their interactions on and off the app some form of authentication would help. You can opt for email login or social media login if you would like to take advantage of some integrations from Twitter and Facebook.

Should users be able to manage profiles?

Will your users be able to interact with each other in anyway and will they be able to modify their presence on their app? It is important to remember that some of this information may be viewed publicly and may have implications on the listing of your app on various app stores.

How should the app make money?

Do you expect users to pay for your application from the app stores or do you expect to make money from in-app purchases. The way in which you monetise your app can have an affect on whether users eventually download your app so it is important to understand your audience if you expect to make money from direct usage or sales of your app.

Will users be able to rate or review things?

Do you have content or products within the app that users will rate or review? Please be aware that the complexity of your rating and review system will have a direct impact on your budget.

Should the app integrate to a website?

Do you have an existing website or service that you will require us to integrate your application to. This will require the building of an API or interface that allows us to communicate with this service or website.

How pretty should your app look?

Building a very beautiful app requires a lot of time and resources which requires a bigger budget. This means that if you want custom animations, buttons and gestures you will need to be prepared to invest more.

Does your app need an icon designed?

Your app will need to be memorable and stand out on the app store and a users device. Designing a pleasing icon requires a bit more money but will help you stand out!
